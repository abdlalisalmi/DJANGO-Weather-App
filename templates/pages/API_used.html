The Yahoo Weather API allows developers to integrate weather information for any location into third-party applications. Available information includes the 10-day forecast, wind, atmospheric conditions, precipitation, sunset time, astronomy conditions, and more. Developers can look up weather by WOEID (Where On Earth IDentifier), city name, or lat/long coordinates.
